## Tokens vs. Words

In generative AI, a **token** is a unit of text that a language model can easily process. Tokens are not always the same as words.

A single word can be broken into multiple tokens. For example:

  - "everyday" > `every` + `day` (2 tokens)
  - "joyful" > `joy` + `ful` (2 tokens)
  - "I'd like" > `I` + `'d` + `like` (3 tokens, same as "I would like")

Breaking words down this way helps models better understand and process the input.

The process of splitting text into tokens is called **tokenization**. Different models use different tokenization methods, and the approach chosen can significantly affect how well the model performs.
